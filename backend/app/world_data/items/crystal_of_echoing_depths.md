# Crystal of Echoing Depths

## Item: crystal_of_echoing_depths
## Type: artifact, treasure, magical_item
## Weight: light
## Value: priceless
## Location: crystal_treasury
## Magic: true

A mesmerizing crystal the size of a human head, composed of what appears to be crystallized starlight. The Crystal of Echoing Depths pulses with an inner blue radiance that seems to respond to the viewer's emotions. When held, it resonates with a barely audible harmonic frequency that creates a sense of profound connection to ancient mysteries.

The crystal's surface is perfectly smooth yet seems to contain infinite depth, as if you're looking into a frozen aurora borealis. Occasionally, fleeting images appear within its depths - glimpses of forgotten civilizations, lost knowledge, and cosmic truths that vanish before they can be fully comprehended.

## Properties
- Provides magical illumination equivalent to bright torchlight
- Resonates with ancient magical energies and hidden knowledge
- Weighs surprisingly little despite its size
- Warm to the touch with a pleasant, calming energy
- Images within suggest it holds memories of the past

## Interaction Effects
- Holding it grants enhanced intuition and perception
- Can detect magical auras and hidden enchantments
- Provides resistance to fear and mental influences  
- May reveal hidden passages or secret mechanisms
- Connects the bearer to the echo of ancient wisdom

## Warnings and Consequences
- Removing it from the treasury triggers a catastrophic cave-in
- Its power makes it highly sought after by dangerous entities
- Prolonged exposure may cause visions that blur reality
- The crystal chooses its bearer and may resist unworthy holders
- Its removal destabilizes the magical protections of the cave system

## Discovery and Examination
- Initial observation reveals its extraordinary beauty and obvious value
- Close examination shows the swirling images and patterns within
- Touching it provides a sense of its benevolent but powerful nature
- Magical investigation reveals its connection to the cave's stability
- Ancient murals provide context for its significance and dangers
