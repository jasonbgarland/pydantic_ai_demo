# World Configuration - Crystal of Echoing Depths Adventure

## Game Scenario Overview
A classic 3-act adventure where players must cross a dangerous chasm, retrieve an ancient crystal treasure, and escape when their action triggers a cave-in trap.

## Room Network

```
[Hidden Alcove]
       |
       | (north/south)
       |
[Cave Entrance] ---(east/west)--- [Yawning Chasm] ---(east/west)--- [Crystal Treasury]
                                         |
                                         | (south/north)
                                         |
                                  [Collapsed Passage]
```

## Act Structure

### Act 1: Getting Across the Chasm
- **Goal**: Navigate from Cave Entrance to Crystal Treasury
- **Challenge**: Cross the dangerous Yawning Chasm safely
- **Resources**: Rope from Cave Entrance, magical climbing gear from Hidden Alcove
- **Skills**: Player choice affects difficulty and safety

### Act 2: Retrieving the Treasure  
- **Goal**: Claim the Crystal of Echoing Depths
- **Challenge**: Understand the consequences and prepare for them
- **Resources**: Information from explorer journal, warnings from murals
- **Decision**: Take crystal knowing it triggers the trap

### Act 3: Escaping the Trap
- **Goal**: Return to Cave Entrance before the cave collapses completely
- **Challenge**: Navigate unstable, collapsing environment under time pressure
- **Resources**: Crystal provides magical light, equipment aids speed
- **Urgency**: Environmental dangers increase over time

## Key Items and Locations

### Starting Equipment (Cave Entrance)
- **Rope**: Basic climbing aid, necessary for chasm crossing
- **Torch**: Light source, can be taken  
- **Leather Pack**: Container for carrying items

### Hidden Cache (Hidden Alcove)
- **Magical Rope**: Enhanced climbing gear, makes chasm crossing safer
- **Climbing Gear**: Pitons, harness, hammer for advanced climbing
- **Healing Potion**: Emergency health restoration
- **Explorer Journal**: Contains crucial warnings and backstory

### The Prize (Crystal Treasury)
- **Crystal of Echoing Depths**: Main treasure, magical artifact
- **Gold Coins**: Minor treasure, freely available
- **Ancient Murals**: Environmental storytelling and warnings

### Environmental Elements
- **Pressure Plate**: Triggers cave-in when crystal is removed
- **Rope Bridge**: Deteriorating crossing mechanism across chasm
- **Rubble and Debris**: Evidence of geological instability

## Game State Progression

### Initial State
- Player starts at Cave Entrance
- All rooms stable and accessible
- Bridge across chasm is worn but functional
- Crystal treasury is pristine and undisturbed

### Mid-Game State  
- Player has crossed chasm (method determines ease of return)
- Hidden alcove may be discovered and looted
- Collapsed passage shows evidence of recent geological activity
- Crystal treasury accessed and treasure obtained

### End-Game State (Post-Crystal Removal)
- Cave-in trap activated throughout the system
- Chasm bridge becomes increasingly unstable
- Additional rockfalls in various rooms create new obstacles
- Time pressure forces quick decision-making and movement

## Challenge Scaling by Character Class

### Warrior
- Higher strength makes rope climbing easier
- Enhanced constitution provides resistance to cave dangers
- Combat skills may help if any guardians are encountered

### Wizard  
- Magical knowledge helps understand crystal properties and warnings
- Spell casting could provide alternative solutions to physical challenges
- Enhanced wisdom helps interpret murals and journal information

### Rogue
- Superior agility makes chasm crossing safer
- Enhanced perception helps discover hidden alcove and secret details
- Stealth and quick thinking aid in rapid escape sequence

## Success Conditions
- **Minimum Success**: Return to cave entrance alive
- **Partial Success**: Escape with some treasure (gold coins)
- **Full Success**: Escape with Crystal of Echoing Depths
- **Perfect Success**: Complete adventure with all party members alive and all treasures recovered

## Environmental Storytelling
- Previous explorers' equipment shows this is an ongoing challenge
- Warning signs and journals provide both help and ominous foreshadowing  
- Geological instability creates a sense of natural danger beyond magical threats
- Murals and crystal suggest ancient civilizations and lost knowledge
